Process

How it works

Discovery
/01

We'll dive deep into your personal goals and long-term vision

1-2 days
  • Initial Consultation:
    I'll understand what matters to clients, goals, and target audience.
  • Research:
    Analyze competitors and industry trends to gather insights.
  • Define Strategy:
    Set design goals, deliverables, and timelines.
Design
/02

I'll create mockups that bring your brand to life

3-5 weeks
  • Wireframing:
    Create low-fidelity sketches to map out the structure.
  • Brand/Visual Creation:
    Develop a design language for branding, colors, fonts, and UI elements.
  • Prototype Development:
    Build clickable prototypes for a client feedback.
  • Product Design:
    Organize the final design with detailed mockups for all pages.
Build
/03

Using code tools, I'll construct your app

2 weeks
  • App Construction:
    Build out the application structure with modern technologies.
  • Content Integration:
    Import and format content (text, images, videos).
  • Resp. SEO Design:
    Optimize app performance for different devices and operating systems.
Launch
/04

Your app goes live, ready to make an impact

1-2 days
  • Client Handover:
    Present the app and train client for handover.
  • Revisions:
    Make necessary changes based on client feedback.
